Ottawa

Canada Tourism and Travel: Best of Canada

Visit Canada’s Parliament Buildings!

Just a short walk from any downtown Ottawa hotel, the Parliament Buildings are home to Canada’s federal government. Located on a scenic tree-covered promontory that overlooks the rolling Ottawa River, the neo-Gothic and Gothic structures were built between 1859 and 1927.

See the Rideau Canal!

Built between 1826 and 1832, the Rideau Canal is Ontario’s only UNESCO World Heritage Site and is a delight to visit in any season!
